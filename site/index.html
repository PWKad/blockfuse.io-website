Why BlockFuse?


Let's be honest. Building applications on top of Litecoin and Bitcoin is difficult. Even if you are a top-notch programmer, you need in-depth protocol level knowledge to adapt a blockchain client to your product. The amount of time to gain this knowledge is an overwhelming barrier for many interested app developers. Here at BlockFuse, we recognize that there is a great need in this space to make this entry point more accessible.


In light of this, we've decided to reduce the technical barrier to entry for non-blockchain developers by offering tools across several reference clients such as litecoind, bitcoind, and even the Lightning Network client lnd. By open sourcing these tools, BlockFuse will open the floodgates for the exponential proliferation of application development for these two currencies.

Values

  • Simplicity

    Reducing the technical barrier to building on top of Litecoin and Bitcoin is of primary importance.
  • Blockchain Agnostic

    Acknowledge that adaptive tools are necessary as the space develops towards a multicoin future.
  • Developer Focused

    Prioritize the needs of developers to make consumer facing applications easier.
  • Open Source

    In the spirit of decentralization, make source code available to be verified through peer review and unrestricted to match the exponential growth of this space.
  • Interoperability

    Ensure applications are compatible with one another across the various reference clients available for Litecoin and Bitcoin.
